The Vial of Life EMR System

Vial of Life


Vial of Life is a lightweight and simple Electronic Medical Record (EMR) system. Before the use of computers was widely spread, people would write down their past medical history and list of medications on a small peice of paper. They would then roll up the peice of paper and put it in a tube to be placed in the freezer. This way when an ill or elderly person had a emergency, EMTs would know to look in the freezer for the patients medical information enclosed in a "Vial of Life".
